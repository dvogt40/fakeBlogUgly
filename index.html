Sarah Bridges

I love this time of year. Fall colors mixed with Halloween decorations. Little did I know the witch costume
I often wear to parties had another significance. My father has spent many years building our family tree.
To the whole families surprise we found out that one of my ancestors was accused of being a witch during
the Salem Witch trials.

Important dates:

Date Event
1675 Sarah's Birthday
1692 Found guilty by the Justices of Salem
1693 Found not guilty.

Important Facts:

  • Born in Andover, Essex, Massachusetts.
  • Sarah was accused for afflicting Martha Sprague.
  • She originally denied the charges.
  • She was pressured to confess and was found guilty.
  • She claimed there were 200 witches in the area.
